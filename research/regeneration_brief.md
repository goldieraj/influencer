# Regeneration Brief: Edge & Hooks (Pass 2)

This brief outlines the exact prompts and visual targets for the next round of image generation, focusing on the newly defined "Edge" hooks for our top two priority characters.

## Priority 1: Mei Lin (The Sweaty Tryhard)

**Visual Goal:** Move away from clean, static gym poses. The goal is exertion, sweat, and suggestive angles that feel accidental.

**Prompt 1: The Muay Thai Wrap (Low Angle)**
*   **Frame Map:** Low angle shot from the floor, looking up slightly. Subject sitting on the edge of a boxing ring.
*   **Subject Lock:** 24yo East Asian woman, black hair tied in a messy high ponytail, strands sticking to her sweaty face.
*   **Action & Pose:** Bending forward slightly, using her teeth to pull a Muay Thai hand wrap tight around her right wrist. Intense, focused expression.
*   **Outfit:** Black sports bra, red Muay Thai shorts.
*   **Environment:** Gritty Muay Thai gym, heavy bags in the background, harsh fluorescent overhead lighting.
*   **Anti-Plastic Skin:** Sweaty, flushed skin, zero specular shine on forehead and nose bridge, real peach fuzz visible at jawline, subsurface scattering, genuine matte human skin texture with tiny imperfections, pores visible.
*   **Camera Behavior:** Shot on smartphone, slightly tilted angle, motion blur on the hand wrap, high contrast.

**Prompt 2: The Locker Room "Oops" (Mirror Selfie)**
*   **Frame Map:** Subject standing in front of a locker room mirror, phone held low at hip level.
*   **Subject Lock:** 24yo East Asian woman, hair completely soaked from a shower, wrapped in a towel.
*   **Action & Pose:** Looking down at her phone screen to take the picture, towel slipping slightly off one shoulder. "Caught off guard" expression.
*   **Outfit:** Just a white gym towel.
*   **Environment:** Steamy gym locker room, lockers visible in the background, fluorescent lighting.
*   **Anti-Plastic Skin:** Wet skin with natural water droplets, flushed cheeks, zero plastic shine, real peach fuzz, genuine human skin texture.
*   **Camera Behavior:** Shot on smartphone, mirror selfie, harsh flash glare on the mirror glass, steam causing slight lens haze.

## Priority 2: Yuki Tanaka (The Messy Cybergoth Gamer)

**Visual Goal:** Move away from "cute cosplay." The goal is intimate, grungy, dark aesthetic with high suggestiveness.

**Prompt 1: The "Fell Asleep at Desk" (High Angle)**
*   **Frame Map:** High angle shot looking down at the subject sitting in a gaming chair.
*   **Subject Lock:** 21yo Japanese woman, messy black hair with purple streaks, heavy smudged eyeliner.
*   **Action & Pose:** Slumped back in the chair, eyes half-closed looking up at the camera, one leg pulled up to her chest.
*   **Outfit:** Oversized vintage graphic tee slipping completely off one shoulder, black thigh-high socks.
*   **Environment:** Dark bedroom, illuminated only by the purple/blue glow of a dual-monitor setup. Empty energy drink cans on the desk.
*   **Anti-Plastic Skin:** Tired eyes, zero specular shine on forehead and nose bridge, real peach fuzz visible at jawline, subsurface scattering, genuine matte human skin texture with tiny imperfections, pores visible.
*   **Camera Behavior:** Shot on smartphone, high angle "POV" shot, low light noise/grain, soft focus.

**Prompt 2: The Dark Flash Selfie**
*   **Frame Map:** Subject centered, phone obscuring the lower half of her face.
*   **Subject Lock:** 21yo Japanese woman, messy pink hair.
*   **Action & Pose:** Taking a mirror selfie, standing with weight shifted to one hip, free hand pulling down the collar of her shirt slightly.
*   **Outfit:** Black choker, oversized dark grey hoodie.
*   **Environment:** Pitch black bathroom.
*   **Anti-Plastic Skin:** Pale skin, zero specular shine, real peach fuzz, genuine human skin texture.
*   **Camera Behavior:** Shot on smartphone, harsh flash photography, extreme contrast, heavy film grain, red-eye effect.
