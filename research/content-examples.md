## KEY PATTERNS FROM TOP CONTENT:

### Content Types Ranked by Engagement:
1. **Activity/Sport Reels** (gym, basketball, yoga) — highest views
2. **Dark/Mystery Aesthetic** — viral potential (25M+ views)
3. **Mirror Selfie Reels** — consistent engagement
4. **Walking/Lifestyle** — steady performers
5. **Question/Engagement Bait** — drives comments
6. **Suggestive but not explicit** — stays on platform

### Caption Formula (from top performers):
1. Start with relatable scenario or question
2. Use casual, conversational tone (lowercase, abbreviations)
3. Include 1-2 emojis (not excessive)
4. End with a question to drive comments
5. Keep it short (2-3 sentences max)

### Reel Format That Works:
- 15-30 seconds optimal
- Single shot, minimal editing
- Activity-based (doing something, not just posing)
- Music/trending audio
- Vertical format (9:16)
