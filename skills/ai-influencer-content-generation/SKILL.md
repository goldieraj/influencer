# AI Influencer Content Generation Skill

Use this skill when the user wants to generate images or video prompts for AI influencer characters (specifically Mei Lin and Yuki Tanaka). This skill incorporates the "Playful Tease" visual formula, "Plausible Deniability" hooks, and AI Cinema techniques for maximum realism and engagement.

## 1. The Core Visual Formula: "Playful Tease"

Do not generate "gritty realism" or "tired/messy" content. The data from 50 top-performing accounts shows the winning formula is:

*   **Lighting:** Natural daylight, golden hour, or soft warm flattering light (no harsh flash or dark rooms).
*   **Skin:** Flawless, glowing, smooth (no visible pores, peach fuzz, or dark circles).
*   **Environment:** Clean, intimate settings (bright bedrooms, clean mirrors, luxury pools, upscale gyms).
*   **Vibe:** Confident, playful, intentional. Direct eye contact is crucial.
*   **Suggestiveness:** 7-8/10. Emphasize curves and body shape through posing and outfit choice, not explicit nudity.

## 2. The Hook Strategy: "Plausible Deniability"

To avoid algorithmic shadowbans while maintaining high suggestiveness, use "Plausible Deniability." The activity must justify the revealing outfit.

*   **The Formula:** Activity + Body in minimal clothing + Simple background
*   **Examples:**
    *   "I'm doing a handstand challenge" (while wearing a tight bodysuit).
    *   "I'm walking in the rain" (while wearing a white shirt).
    *   "I'm stretching for Muay Thai" (while bending over in shorts).

## 4. Prompting Structure (The AI Cinema Grammar)

When generating prompts for Midjourney, Flux, or ComfyUI, use this exact structure:

`[Frame Map] + [Subject Lock] + [Anti-Plastic Skin] + [Camera Behavior]`

1.  **Frame Map:** Define the shot type and environment. (e.g., "Medium-wide shot, locker room mirror selfie, warm overhead lighting.")
2.  **Subject Lock:** Describe the character using their specific traits. (e.g., "24-year-old East Asian woman, long straight black hair, athletic slim-thick build.")
3.  **Anti-Plastic Skin:** Force realism without making it gritty. (e.g., "Flawless glowing skin, natural skin texture, subtle flush, highly detailed.")
4.  **Camera Behavior:** Define the lens and film stock. (e.g., "Shot on iPhone 14 Pro, 24mm lens, slight film grain, candid snapshot.")

## 6. Output Generation

When the user asks for prompts or image concepts, output 3-5 distinct concepts using the Prompting Structure (Section 4), ensuring they align with the Playful Tease formula and Plausible Deniability hooks. Specify the intended platform (Instagram Grid vs. Twitter Uncensored).
