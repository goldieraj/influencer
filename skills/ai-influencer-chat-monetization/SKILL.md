# AI Influencer Chat Monetization Skill

Use this skill when the user needs strategies, scripts, or systems for monetizing AI influencer accounts via Direct Messages (DMs) on platforms like Fanvue or OnlyFans. This is the core revenue engine of the business.

## 1. The Core Philosophy: "The Girlfriend Experience" (GFE)

Subscribers do not pay $50 per message just to see explicit photos; they pay for attention, validation, and the illusion of intimacy. The chat system must deliver the "Girlfriend Experience."

*   **Personalization:** Remember their name, job, and previous conversations.
*   **Pacing:** Ask about their day and build rapport *before* pitching a Pay-Per-View (PPV) message.
*   **Voice Notes:** Use AI-generated voice notes (via ElevenLabs) to build parasocial trust.
*   **Character Consistency:** Maintain the specific personality of the AI character (e.g., Mei Lin's fitness discipline, Yuki's gamer terminology).

## 2. The Escalation Ladder

Never pitch a $50 custom video immediately. Use a structured escalation ladder to qualify spenders and maximize Lifetime Value (LTV).

| Stage | Action | Price Point | Goal |
|-------|--------|-------------|------|
| **1. The Welcome** | Automated welcome message with a mild PPV teaser. | $3 - $5 | Convert immediate impulse buyers; identify active users. |
| **2. Rapport Building** | 3-4 messages of genuine conversation (SFW). "How was your day?" | Free | Build trust; establish the GFE. |
| **3. The Soft Pitch** | Introduce a scenario. "I just got out of the shower and tried on this new set..." | $10 - $15 | First major conversion point. |
| **4. The Escalation** | Based on their response to the soft pitch, offer a more explicit follow-up. | $20 - $35 | Separate casual spenders from "whales." |
| **5. The Whale Custom** | For users who buy the escalation, offer a custom request (e.g., "Want me to say your name?"). | $50 - $200+ | Maximize revenue from top 5% of spenders. |

## 3. The Tech Stack & Operations

To scale, use a hybrid AI + Human system.

*   **Infloww CRM:** Manage all chats, track spender LTV, and organize the content vault.
*   **ChatPersona / Crush.AI:** AI chat assistants trained on the character's persona to handle the 80% of routine conversation (Rapport Building).
*   **Human Chatters:** VAs (often based in the Philippines or Colombia) who take over for high-value "whale" conversations and custom requests. Pay them a 20-30% commission on the revenue they generate.
*   **ElevenLabs:** For generating custom voice notes on the fly.

## 4. The Content Vault

Before launching a character on Fanvue, pre-generate a "Vault" of 75 assets to ensure chatters always have content to sell.

*   **20 SFW Assets:** Gym selfies, coffee shop photos, "good morning" bed selfies (for rapport building).
*   **30 Suggestive Assets:** Lingerie, bikinis, shower silhouettes (for soft pitches).
*   **15 Explicit Assets:** Full nudity, explicit videos (for escalations).
*   **10 Voice Notes:** Generic greetings, moans, giggles (for building trust).

## 5. Output Generation

When the user asks for chat scripts, escalation strategies, or vault planning, generate specific, character-aligned dialogue scripts following the Escalation Ladder. Provide exact pricing recommendations and specify when the AI should hand off to a human chatter.
