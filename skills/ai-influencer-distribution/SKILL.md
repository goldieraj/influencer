# AI Influencer Distribution & Growth Skill

Use this skill when the user needs strategies for posting content, growing accounts, managing multiple platforms, or avoiding shadowbans for AI influencer characters.

## 1. The Multi-Platform Funnel

Organic Instagram alone is insufficient. The goal is to drive high-intent traffic from multiple platforms into a link aggregator (like Bouncy.ai), which then funnels to a free Telegram channel, and finally to the paid Fanvue/OnlyFans subscription.

### Platform Roles
*   **TikTok (Top of Funnel):** Viral discovery. SFW video only (trending audio, lip-syncs). Suggestiveness: 2-3/10. Goal: Drive traffic to Instagram.
*   **Instagram (Mid Funnel):** Credibility and primary funnel. Plausible deniability reels (3-5 per day). Suggestiveness: 5-6/10. Goal: Drive traffic to the link in bio.
*   **Reddit (High-Intent Traffic):** Niche targeting. Static images in specific subreddits (e.g., r/asianfitgirls for Mei Lin). Suggestiveness: 7-8/10. Goal: Direct traffic to the link in the Reddit bio.
*   **Twitter/X (Uncensored Funnel):** Engagement networks. Static images, highly suggestive "tease" posts. Suggestiveness: 8-9/10. Goal: Direct traffic to Fanvue.
*   **Telegram (Retention):** Free VIP channel. Blurred teasers, voice notes, flash sales. Suggestiveness: 7-8/10. Goal: Convert lurkers to paid subscribers.

## 2. Instagram & TikTok Growth Tactics

### The 60/40 Rule (Personality vs. Body)
Based on top-performing accounts (like AthenaParissss), the content mix must be 60% personality/humor/lifestyle and 40% body/thirst traps. Pure thirst traps get shadowbanned; personality content builds parasocial connection.

### Volume is King
Post 3-5 Reels/TikToks per day, spaced 3-4 hours apart. Do not rely on static images for growth; use video.

### Weather & Element Hooks
Videos featuring rain, snow, or wind perform exceptionally well. (e.g., "Walking in the rain" while wearing a white shirt).

### Ironic/Self-Aware Captions
Do not use generic AI captions. Use POV text overlays with trending audio. Example: "POV: You're at the gym but you can't stop staring at the girl in the green set."

## 3. Ban Avoidance & Redundancy

AI influencer accounts face high ban rates. Implement these safety measures:

*   **No Direct OF/Fanvue Links:** Never put a direct adult link in an Instagram or TikTok bio. Always use a safe aggregator like Bouncy.ai or Linktree.
*   **Plausible Deniability:** Always provide a "legitimate reason" for revealing content (sports, stretching, trying on clothes).
*   **Infrastructure Isolation:** Use an antidetect browser (AdsPower) and dedicated 4G/Residential proxies for each character. Never log into multiple character accounts from the same IP/device fingerprint.
*   **Backup Accounts:** Always run a "backup" Instagram account simultaneously. Direct followers to the backup account in Stories ("Follow my backup in case this gets deleted!").
