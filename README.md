## Critical Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. **NEVER put "AI" in username or bio** — accounts that do get banned
2. **Character consistency is everything** — same face every post or audience leaves
3. **Volume beats perfection** — 2-3 posts/day, throw away 80% of generations
4. **Comment farming is the #1 growth lever** — not posting, COMMENTING
5. **Fanvue not OnlyFans** — OF banned AI creators in 2026
6. **Backup accounts always ready** — bans happen, be prepared
7. **Personality beats beauty** — quirks, humor, and attitude build parasocial bonds
8. **The "edit bay" is 30% of realism** — AI gets you to 70%, post-production does the rest
