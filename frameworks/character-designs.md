# AI Influencer Character Designs

This document defines the 2 core AI influencer characters designed to operate as a network. Each character targets a different audience segment, aesthetic, and content niche — allowing cross-promotion without cannibalization.

---

## Character 1: "Mei Lin" — The Flawless Gym Crush (Asian Fitness)

**Positioning:** An evolution of Aiko.Asia's model, optimized using the "Playful Tease" formula. East Asian (Chinese/Korean mixed), 24 years old, based in "Los Angeles." The core hook is the *flawless gym crush*. Clean, bright gyms. Stylish athleisure. Glowing skin. Confident smile at the camera.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Ethnicity | East Asian (Chinese/Korean features) |
| Age | 24 |
| Location | Los Angeles, CA |
| Body Type | Athletic, toned, slim-thick |
| Hair | Long, straight, black. Well-styled even in the gym. |
| Signature Look | Matching high-end athleisure sets (sage green, black), bodycon dresses |
| The "Edge" / Hook | Plausible deniability (stretching, sports) combined with flawless skin, warm lighting, and a confident, inviting gaze. |
| Content Mix | 60% fitness/gym (suggestive angles but safe context), 25% lifestyle/golden hour, 15% mirror outfit checks |
| Monetization | Fanvue (gym crush → intimate bedroom → explicit escalation) |
| Comparable Accounts | @aiko.asia, @joanne.kisses, @deanna.ritter |

**LoRA Training Notes:** Train on 40-50 images showing varied but *flattering* lighting (golden hour outdoor, bright clean gym, well-lit bathroom mirror). Include at least 10 close-up face shots, 15 full-body gym poses, 10 swimwear/beach, 5 casual lifestyle. Maintain consistent facial features: high cheekbones, almond eyes, natural brows, flawless glowing skin.

## Character 2: "Yuki Tanaka" — The Neon Dream Girl (E-Girl/Gamer)

**Positioning:** Japanese aesthetic, 21 years old, based in "Tokyo." This is NOT a gritty, tired gamer. The hook is "The Neon Dream Girl." Vibrant clean neon lighting. Cute, slightly revealing outfits (oversized band tees as dresses, crop tops). Flawless makeup. Playful, inviting expression. The "Playful Tease" formula applied to the gamer aesthetic.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Ethnicity | Japanese |
| Age | 21 |
| Location | Tokyo, Japan |
| Body Type | Petite, slim |
| Hair | Changes frequently (pink, purple, black), but always clean and styled |
| Signature Look | Oversized graphic tees (as dresses), crop tops, thigh-highs, clean aesthetic setups |
| The "Edge" / Hook | Intimate but clean bedroom/desk shots, neon glow, mirror selfies, playful and inviting expressions |
| Content Mix | 50% aesthetic gaming setup/bedroom, 30% mirror selfies/outfit checks, 20% cafe/lifestyle |
| Monetization | Fanvue (neon gamer → intimate bedroom → explicit escalation) |
| Comparable Accounts | @lilithhberry (for the bedroom style), top-tier e-girl accounts |

**LoRA Training Notes:** Train on 40 images mixing hyperrealistic face with flawless skin. Include 10 close-ups (various hair colors), 10 aesthetic gaming/neon setups, 10 casual/bedroom, 10 fashion. The hair color changes are a FEATURE — each color can be a different "era" that drives engagement. Ensure lighting is always flattering (neon pinks/purples, warm morning light).

These 2 characters should interact as if they are real friends who met through modeling/social media:
