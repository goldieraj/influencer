# 30-Day Content Calendar Template

This 30-day template is designed to maximize algorithmic reach on Instagram and TikTok while driving high-intent traffic to the Fanvue funnel. It balances the "Growth/Reach" content (safe, relatable, shareable) with "Conversion" content (suggestive, intimate, direct CTA).

## Core Posting Rhythm
- **Feed Posts:** 1 per day (alternating Reels and Statics/Carousels)
- **Stories:** 3-5 per day (essential for funnel conversion)
- **Posting Time:** 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM or 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM in the character's stated timezone.

## Daily Story Framework (The Conversion Engine)

Feed posts get them to look; Stories get them to click the link in bio.

**Post 3-5 Stories daily using this arc:**
1. **Morning (9 AM):** Casual "Good morning" mirror selfie or coffee cup shot. (Builds habit/routine).
2. **Mid-day (1 PM):** A poll or question box. (e.g., "Gym or rest day?", "What should I have for lunch?"). (Drives engagement).
3. **Afternoon (4 PM):** A lifestyle shot or repost of a feed post.
4. **Evening (8 PM - The Hook):** A slightly suggestive, intimate, or "exclusive" looking image.
5. **Night (9 PM - The Pitch):** Text overlay on a dark or blurry image: "Uploading the rest of this set to my exclusive page right now... link in bio to see it before it's gone 🤫🤍"

## Hashtag Strategy
- Use 3-5 highly relevant, medium-density hashtags per post.
- Do not use #AI, #AIart, or #AIinfluencer. The goal is to pass as human.
- **Example for Mei Lin:** `#fitnessjourney #gymgirl #losangeleslife #workoutmotivation`
- **Example for Yuki Tanaka:** `#kawaiifashion #tokyostyle #gamergirl #cosplayaesthetic`
