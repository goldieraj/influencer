# Chat Monetization SOP: The Revenue Engine

The Instagram/TikTok content is just the top of the funnel. The actual revenue in the AI Influencer business model comes from Direct Messages (DMs) on Fanvue/OnlyFans. This document outlines the exact system for turning subscribers into high-paying "whales" through structured chat escalation.

## 1. The Core Philosophy: "The Girlfriend Experience" (GFE)

Subscribers do not pay $50/message just to see explicit photos; they can find that for free elsewhere. They pay for **attention, validation, and the illusion of intimacy**.

Your chatters (or AI chat system) must deliver the "Girlfriend Experience" (GFE). This means:
- Remembering their name, job, and previous conversations.
- Asking about their day before pitching a pay-per-view (PPV) message.
- Using voice notes (AI-generated) to build parasocial trust.
- Maintaining the character's specific personality (e.g., Mei Lin's fitness discipline, Yuki's gamer terminology).

## 2. The Tech Stack

To scale this across multiple characters, you cannot chat manually. You must use a hybrid AI + Human system.

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| **Infloww / Supercreator** | CRM and chat management. Tracks which subscribers are "whales" vs "freeloaders." | ~$30-50/mo |
| **ChatPersona / Crush.AI** | AI chatbots trained on your character's personality to handle the initial 80% of mundane "how are you" messages. | Varies |
| **ElevenLabs / PlayHT** | Voice cloning. Use this to send custom voice notes ("Hey [Name], I'm thinking about you"). | ~$22/mo |
| **Human Chatters** | VAs (typically from the Philippines/Colombia) who take over the AI when a high-value sales opportunity arises. | 20-30% Commission |

## 4. The Chatter Hiring & Commission Structure

When you hit 100+ active subscribers, you must hire human chatters to manage the volume 24/7 in shifts. Chatters typically work on a commission-only basis, earning between 20% and 30% of the Net Revenue they generate from PPV and tips. To maintain continuous coverage, hire three chatters to cover 8-hour shifts (e.g., US Morning, US Evening, US Night), keeping in mind that the highest revenue window occurs between 9 PM and 2 AM EST. You must provide them with comprehensive training, including this SOP, the Character Design document, and access to the content vault.

## 5. The "Vault" System

Chatters cannot wait 10 minutes for you to generate an image mid-conversation. You must build a comprehensive "Vault" of pre-generated content organized in a shared Google Drive or directly within the Infloww CRM.

The standard vault inventory for a new character includes:

| Content Type | Quantity Required | Primary Use Case |
|--------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Casual/SFW Selfies** | 20 assets | Used for daily rapport building, "good morning" messages, and maintaining the girlfriend illusion. |
| **Lingerie/Suggestive** | 30 assets | Used for the "Soft Pitch" phase and low-ticket ($5-$15) PPV unlocks. |
| **Implied Explicit** | 15 assets | Reserved for high-ticket ($25+) PPV unlocks during active sexting sessions. |
| **Generic Voice Notes** | 10 assets | Used to verify identity and build intimacy (e.g., "Good morning baby," "I miss you"). |
