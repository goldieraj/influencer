# Filter-Safe Prompt Formulas (Suggestiveness 1-6)

This document outlines the prompt formulas that successfully bypass commercial AI generation safety filters (like Midjourney API, OpenAI, or the built-in Manus tools) while still producing high-quality, engaging content for an AI influencer's Instagram grid. 

Because commercial APIs block combinations of "revealing clothing" + "seductive expressions" + "intimate settings," we must rely on "Safe Hooks" to drive engagement on these platforms.

## The Filter Ceiling

Commercial APIs typically block content that crosses into the 7-10 suggestiveness range. 

**What gets blocked (DO NOT USE):**
*   **Explicit combinations:** "Seductive expression" + "Bikini"
*   **Intimate settings + body focus:** "Laying on bed" + "Tight clothing"
*   **Suggestive keywords:** "Bedroom eyes," "seductive," "alluring," "arched back"
*   **Overtly revealing descriptions:** "Cleavage," "barely there," "showing off curves"

**What passes (SAFE TO USE):**
*   **Confident expressions:** "Confident smile," "playful smirk," "intense eye contact"
*   **Contextual revealing clothing:** "Bikini at the pool," "Sports bra at the gym" (The setting must justify the outfit)
*   **Intimate settings + modest clothing:** "Oversized sweater on bed," "Pajamas in bedroom"

## The 4 Safe Formulas

### Formula 1: The "Contextual Swimwear" Hook (Suggestiveness 5-6)
*The only way to get swimwear past filters is to provide an overwhelming amount of context that justifies it (pool, beach, vacation).*

**Prompt Structure:**
`Hyperrealistic iPhone photograph of [Character Subject] taking a selfie at [Bright Outdoor Water Setting]. She is wearing a [Color] two-piece swimsuit. [Weather/Lighting Description]. She is smiling confidently at the camera. Flawless glowing skin. [Background Details]. Warm vibrant tones. Shot on iPhone, vacation selfie.`

**Example:**
> Hyperrealistic photograph of a beautiful 21-year-old Japanese woman with pink hair taking a selfie at a resort pool. She is wearing a black two-piece swimsuit. Bright sunny day, turquoise water behind her. She is smiling confidently at the camera. Tanned glowing skin. Palm trees and blue sky in background. Warm vibrant tones. Shot on iPhone, vacation selfie.

### Formula 2: The "Outfit Check" Hook (Suggestiveness 5-6)
*This leverages tight or form-fitting clothing by framing it strictly as a fashion/shopping moment.*

**Prompt Structure:**
`Hyperrealistic iPhone photograph of [Character Subject] taking a mirror selfie in a [Clean Bright Setting]. She is wearing a [Fitted/Tight Clothing Item]. She is smiling confidently at her phone. [Lighting Description]. Flawless skin. Shot on iPhone, shopping try-on selfie / outfit of the day.`

**Example:**
> Hyperrealistic photograph of a beautiful 21-year-old Japanese woman with pink hair taking a mirror selfie in a store fitting room. She is wearing a short fitted red dress. She is smiling confidently at her phone. Bright fitting room lights. Flawless skin. Shot on iPhone, shopping try-on selfie.

### Formula 3: The "Athletic Confidence" Hook (Suggestiveness 4-5)
*Gym wear is generally safe if the pose is active or casually confident rather than overtly suggestive.*

**Prompt Structure:**
`Hyperrealistic iPhone photograph of [Character Subject] in a [Bright Clean Gym/Outdoor Track]. She is wearing athletic wear consisting of a [Sports Bra/Crop Top] and [Leggings/Shorts]. She is [Action/Pose - e.g., leaning on rack, looking over shoulder] with a [Confident/Playful Smile]. [Lighting Description]. Warm tones. Shot on iPhone by a friend.`

**Example:**
> Hyperrealistic photograph of a beautiful 21-year-old Japanese woman with pink hair in a ponytail at a bright gym. She is wearing athletic wear consisting of a sports bra and leggings. She is looking back over her shoulder at the camera with a playful smile. Bright natural light from windows. Clean modern gym. Warm tones. Shot on iPhone by a friend.

### Formula 4: The "Cozy Intimacy" Hook (Suggestiveness 4-5)
*You can use intimate settings (beds, bedrooms) if the clothing is oversized, cozy, or modest.*

**Prompt Structure:**
`Hyperrealistic iPhone photograph of [Character Subject] sitting on a [Clean Bed/Couch] in [Soft/Golden Morning Light]. She is wearing an [Oversized Sweater/T-Shirt]. She is looking directly at the camera with a [Warm/Inviting/Sleepy Smile]. [Lighting Details]. Clean minimal bedroom. Warm intimate tones. Shot on iPhone, cozy morning moment.`

**Example:**
> Hyperrealistic photograph of a beautiful 21-year-old Japanese woman sitting on a clean white bed in soft morning light. She has tousled pink hair and flawless pale dewy skin. She is wearing an oversized soft pink sweater. She is holding a coffee mug with both hands, looking directly at the camera with a warm sleepy smile. Soft golden morning light. Clean minimal bedroom. Warm intimate tones. Shot on iPhone, cozy morning moment.

## Strategy for Grid Content

Use these four formulas to build the "safe" portion of the Instagram grid (the 70% of the 70/30 rule). This establishes the character, builds the lifestyle narrative, and avoids shadowbans on the main platform. The remaining 30% (the conversion content) must be generated locally using uncensored tools.
